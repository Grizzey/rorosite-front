Vision and Mission

To be the leading provider of safe, reliable and most of all comfortable transportation for everyone.
And setting the standard for excellence in traveling whatever island you like in the Philippines.

Our mission is to ensure the safety and peace of mind of our every passenger by Montecillo Roro transportation services.
We will provide and maintain a high safety standards for everybody who will be riding with us.

About us

Captain Mangji Hasa Montecillio founded Montecillio Roro in 1985.
Captain Montecillio was a talented Filipino mariner who dreamed of enhancing inter-island transport within the Philippines.
